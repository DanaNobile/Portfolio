Digital Radiography

The Rising Stars Dental team utilizes the most advanced technologies to identify potential problems that cannot be seen by clinical examination. Our team uses digital x-rays to minimize the radiation exposure to your child. We use protective lead aprons for the body and thyroid to enhance your child's safety.

Whitening

We know you are invested in your child's smile. The specialists at Rising Stars Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics are committed to healthy and bright smiles. Our team will help you choose the appropriate whitening technique while minimizing sensitivity to young teeth.

Mouth Guards (and Night Guards)

It is very important for your athlete to stay safe while practicing sports. The Rising Stars Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics team offers properly fitted mouth guards to reduce the severity of sports-related mouth and teeth injuries.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Children might have certain conditions that affect the formation of their teeth which cause discoloration, defective development or lack of development. Rising Stars Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics specialists work together to provide a comprehensive plan to restore the healthiest smile using the most advanced treatments. Children do not have to wait until adulthood to have a perfect smile.
