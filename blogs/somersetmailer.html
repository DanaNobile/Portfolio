At Somerset Family Dental, we are dedicated to providing high-quality dentistry with a personal touch to your entire family. We understand that a healthy smile can give you the self-confidence you deserve and set the foundation for a lifetime of dental health.

Using the latest advances in dental technology, our goal is to make all of your visits comfortable and enjoyable, while we help you achieve the healthiest, most attractive smile possible. Call us today to schedule an appointment with Dr. Candice Grams!

Back

Dr. Candice Grams was born in Johannesburg, South Africa and raised in Boca Raton, Florida. She graduated Magna Cum Laude in Exercise Science from Florida State University and then went on to complete her dental degree at Midwestern University, Arizona.

“For many years, I have had the privilege and honor to care for hundreds of patients, and I am blessed to continue with my legacy to offer the highest level of skill and compassion for all my patients. As a dentist, my commitment is that our friendly and well-trained team provides you and your family with the highest quality dental care you can depend on.”

As many current patients of Dr. Grams have experienced firsthand, compassion and extra time spent with each patient is her top priority, providing each and every one a smile they can feel proud of.

In her spare time, she enjoys traveling, exercising, hiking, and giving back to the community by volunteering at Mission of Mercy. Her passion for dentistry and commitment to her patients empowers her to expand her knowledge in the dental field by frequently attending CE courses.

“I believe that your needs come first, and our team will do whatever it takes to ensure you have a wonderful experience in our care.”
